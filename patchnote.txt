--- General ---

Now support translation.
Added a refresh for Fanny Packs XL because some balance was made.
Refresh crafting category change from Tailoring to General.
Added compatibility with Better Belts mod, you can now use your upgraded belts to craft fanny packs.
You can now use holsters to craft fanny packs.

--- Balance changes ---

Classic Fanny Packs :
Item was a little overpowered.
    - Tailoring level required to craft : Nerf from 1 to 2
    - Weight : Nerf from 0.1 to 0.2
    - Run Speed Modifier = Nerf from 0.99 to 0.97

Sports Fanny Pack :
Lower capacity, < run speed, > weight reduction.
    - Tailoring level required to craft : Nerf from 2 to 3

Fanny Pack XL :
More capacity, > run speed, ~ weight reduction.
    - Tailoring level required to craft : Nerf from 2 to 3
    - Weight = Nerf from 0.1 to 0.3
    - RunSpeedModifier = Reverted from 0.99 to 0.95
