description=- weight reduction increased to 80 from 7,

description=1) Sports fanny pack - capacity 2, weight reduction 90%
description=2) Fanny pack XL - capacity 4, weight reduction 70%
description=

description=Mod will not change already spawned items. In order to update your existing Fanny Pack:
description=1) Take out any items inside your fanny pack
