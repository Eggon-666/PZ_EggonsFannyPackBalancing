title=Eggon's Fanny Pack Balancing
description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=This mod tweaks fanny pack to make it more usable:
description=1) weight decreased to 0.1 from 1.0,
description=2) weight reduction increased to 80 from 7,
description=3) run speed modifier increased to 0.99 from 0.97.
description=It also adds recipe for fanny pack (requires Tailoring 1) and 2 additional fanny packs (require Tailoring 2):
description=1) Sports fanny pack - capacity 2, weight reduction 90%
description=2) Fanny pack XL - capacity 4, weight reduction 70%
description=[h3]Existing saves[/h3]
description=Mod will not change already spawned items. In order to update your existing Fanny Pack:
description=1) Take out any items inside your fanny pack
description=2) Right click on the fanny pack and click the "Refresh Fanny Pack" option from context menu.
description=[h3]Compatibility[/h3]
description=This is a very simple mod affecting just 2 items. I do not expect any compatibility issues. If you find any, please let me know.
